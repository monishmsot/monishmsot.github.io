FAQs
What are the modes through which training is available for this course?
  • Training delivery mode is of 2 types:
  • 1. Classroom Training
  • 2. Live Virtual Classroom Training
  • Please refer to the mode of training section in the course description for details.
What is classroom training?
  • Classroom training is an in-person instructor-led training conducted in a classroom or any physical environment.
What is LVC training?
  • In Live Virtual Classroom (LVC) training, the live instructor imparts training in an interactive way through online mode or virtual environment.
Do you offer self-paced training?
  • No, currently we do not offer self-paced learning.
Do you offer customized training to fit a specific industry or business unit?
  • Yes, we do offer customized training programs to fit any specific industry or as per business unit requirements. There are two types of customization; course design customization and course content customization.
  • For course design customization, please contact our support team for details.
  • The extent of course content customization varies as per the service band selected by the business requester. For more details, please refer to Service band contextualization on the pre-cart page.
What should a participant refer to before attending the course?
  • A participant should refer to the following:
  • 1) Pre-read (if shared any)
  • 2) Prerequisites (If any)
  • 3) Course Objectives
Do the participants have to meet the prerequisites before attending the course?
  • Yes, it is necessary. Prerequisites ensure the preparedness and understanding of participants on the foundation skills required to pursue the course.
  • The participants enrolled in a course in which they have not met the prerequisites, gain little to no knowledge on the subject. It may also hinder the learning experiences of other participants in the course.
Is there a pre-connect with participants before training?
  • Yes, participant pre-connect is part of the service band feature. Currently, it is applicable from the Gold service band onwards. Kindly refer to the service band to avail of this service.
Is there a post-connect with participants after training?
  • Yes, participant post-connect is part of the Diamond service band. Kindly refer to the service band to avail of this service.
What happens in a pre-connect?
  • In a pre-connect, the trainer/facilitator connects with their participants before the scheduled training. It is focused on rapport building and setting the objectives for the training or the program. The trainer comes to understand the expectations of the trainees and can modify the training process if necessary.
What happens in a post-connect?
  • Post-connect is meant for doubt clearance. The trainer/facilitator clarifies any questions or concerns of the participants relating to the training session.
Will the participants receive a certificate of completion after attending the course?
  • Yes, each participant will receive a certificate of completion post attending their training through Ordertrainings.
